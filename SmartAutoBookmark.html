Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

Overview

Smart Auto Bookmark ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Bookmarks: URLs and titles of pages you bookmark (stored in Chrome's native bookmark system)
  • Tags: Auto-generated tags for your bookmarks
  • Settings: Your preferences (time threshold, folder name, feature toggles)

All data is stored using Chrome's built-in storage APIs and remains on your device.

Permissions Used

  • tabs: To get the URL and title of pages for bookmarking
  • bookmarks: To create and manage bookmarks
  • storage: To save your settings locally
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click bookmark option
  • scripting: To extract page metadata for auto-tagging
  • alarms: To schedule bookmark organization tasks
  • host permissions: To track browsing time on web pages

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. All data remains on your local device.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your browser's built-in security mechanisms. We do not have access to your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please create an issue on our GitHub repository.
